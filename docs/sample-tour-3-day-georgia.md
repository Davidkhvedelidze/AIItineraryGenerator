## Overview

This 3-day private Georgia tour from Tbilisi is designed for travelers who want to see the country's main highlights in a short but comfortable itinerary.

During the tour, travelers explore Tbilisi, Georgia's historic capital, visit Mtskheta, discover the Kakheti wine region, and travel through Ananuri, Gudauri, and Kazbegi for mountain views and scenic landscapes.

The tour includes private transportation, an air-conditioned vehicle, bottled water, WiFi on board, airport transfers, and driver-guide service. The route is flexible and can be adjusted based on travel style, family needs, weather, and timing.

This tour is especially suitable for first-time visitors, families, couples, small groups, and travelers from Gulf countries who prefer private transport, flexible stops, and comfortable pacing.
